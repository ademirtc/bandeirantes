typedef struct gft::Scene32::_scene32 gft::Scene32::Scene32

It supports both linear and three-dimensional access (i.e., scn->data[p] or scn->array[z][y][x] for a voxel (x,y,z) at address p=x+y*xsize+z*xsize*ysize).
